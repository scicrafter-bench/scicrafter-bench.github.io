SciCrafter

A benchmark for evaluating LLM-based agents on the full scientific research workflow — from literature review and hypothesis generation to experiment design, execution, and paper writing.

Overview

SciCrafter provides a comprehensive evaluation framework that tests whether AI agents can autonomously perform end-to-end scientific research tasks. The benchmark covers multiple disciplines and measures both process quality and outcome validity.
